Statistical Error Rates and Units of Analysis
Unit of Analysis Type Notation Definition
Experiment, $e$ Type I $\alpha_e$ The rate of falsely rejecting the null hypothesis ($d=0$) for that single test (used in aggregation).
All experiments in a study, $s$ Family-Wise $\alpha_s$ The mean Type I Error Rate across all conditions in that specific study.
All Experiments Family-Wise $\alpha_{\text{E}}$ The mean Type I Error Rate across all conditions identified as plausibly spurious.
